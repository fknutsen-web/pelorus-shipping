How we work

Independent by design.

We represent cargo owners — and only cargo owners. That independence is what makes our advice worth having.

01

No freight spreads

We don't trade freight or take the other side of your cargo. We buy freight for you, never from you.

02

No hidden commissions

No carrier commissions, terminal rebates or referral fees. Our only revenue is the fee you agree with us.

03

No asset bias

We own no vessels, terminals or warehouses, so no recommendation is ever driven by filling our own capacity.

04

Transparent alignment

One interest only — yours. Every decision is measured against commercial performance, not utilisation.

05

Best execution

We coordinate the strongest execution partners in the market rather than selling you our own.

06

One point of responsibility

From a single voyage to a full COA programme, one desk owns the commercial outcome end to end.
