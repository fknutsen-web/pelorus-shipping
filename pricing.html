    Why we work differently

    Built for long-term partnerships.

    Meaningful supply chain improvements are not achieved through isolated freight transactions.

    Pelorus is designed around long-term commercial partnerships. Rather than competing for individual shipments, we become an extension of our clients' commercial organisations — providing ongoing freight procurement, contract management, market intelligence and strategic oversight.

    Our objective is to continuously improve transportation performance, reduce commercial risk and protect supply chain margin over time.

    For this reason, our Commercial Freight Desk and Freight Program Management engagements typically begin with a minimum 12-month commitment.

    How we work

    Independent by design.

    We represent cargo owners — and only cargo owners. That independence is what makes our advice worth having.

    01

    No freight spreads

    We don't trade freight or take the other side of your cargo. We buy freight for you, never from you.

    02

    No hidden commissions

    No carrier commissions, terminal rebates or referral fees. Our only revenue is the fee you agree with us.

    03

    No asset bias

    We own no vessels, terminals or warehouses, so no recommendation is ever driven by filling our own capacity.

    04

    Transparent alignment

    One interest only — yours. Every decision is measured against commercial performance, not utilisation.

    05

    Best execution

    We coordinate the strongest execution partners in the market rather than selling you our own.

    06

    One point of responsibility

    Across every programme, contract and mode, one desk owns the commercial outcome over the life of the relationship.
