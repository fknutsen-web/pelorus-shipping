How we work

The model is the difference.

Anyone can fix a vessel. What sets Pelorus apart is whose side we're on — and how we're paid.

01

Embedded & exclusive

Your standing freight desk, not a voyage-by-voyage vendor — and exclusive to you within your lanes.

02

A fixed per-ton fee

A set fee per freight ton, never a percentage of the bill. Our only incentive is to drive your freight down.

03

Never a position against you

We don't trade freight or take the other side of your cargo. Pure representation of the cargo owner, start to finish.
