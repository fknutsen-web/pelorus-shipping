The brief

More than a broker. Less than a full internal department.

Fredheim Logistics provides outsourced commercial freight management for companies that move cargo but do not want to build a full internal freight desk.

We help cargo owners, purchasers, traders, terminals, and industrial companies manage ocean, barge, and inland freight with commercial discipline and experienced oversight — structuring freight properly, protecting margin, and reducing execution risk.

Why it matters

Freight is often the largest controllable cost in the supply chain — and one of the easiest places to quietly erode margin.

Poor structure, weak market timing, unclear responsibilities, and unmanaged voyage execution can turn profitable cargo into a loss.

Fredheim gives you access to senior commercial freight experience — applied to your specific cargo, lanes, and contracts — without the fixed cost of building it in-house.

The result: freight structured properly, margin protected, execution risk reduced, and better commercial decisions made with confidence.
